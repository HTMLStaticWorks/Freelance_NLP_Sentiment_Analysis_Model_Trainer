Technical FAQ

How do you handle sarcasm and double-negation?
We utilize multi-head attention mechanisms that are specifically fine-tuned on datasets containing rhetorical linguistic features. Our sarcasm layer adds a secondary evaluation pass to identify conflicting sentiment markers.
What industries are currently supported for adaptation?
We maintain pre-trained pivot features for Finance, Healthcare, Legal, and E-commerce. Custom industries can be adapted within 72 hours using our continual pre-training pipelines.
Is my data used to train global models?
No. We follow a strict Zero-Knowledge architecture. Your data is used exclusively to create your private domain-adapted weights, which are isolated and encrypted at rest.
