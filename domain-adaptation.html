SEMANTIC DRIFT

Bridging the Contextual Gap

Language is fluid. A model trained on Amazon product reviews will fail to detect sentiment in a Central Bank interest rate announcement because the linguistic markers of "success" or "failure" have completely shifted.

METHODOLOGY

The Adaptation Workflow

Continual Pre-training

Further pre-training the base model on millions of unlabeled target-domain tokens to learn the unique vocabulary.

Adversarial Alignment

Using a Domain Discriminator to ensure that the internal model representations are domain-invariant.

Pivot Selection

Identifying "pivot" features that behave similarly in both domains to ground the sentiment transfer variable.
