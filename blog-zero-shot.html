Zero-Shot Sentiment for Global Dialects

By Dr. Sarah Chen • April 28, 2026

How do you predict sentiment for a language with zero labeled examples? We look at the power of cross-lingual transfer learning.

Cross-Lingual Embeddings

By mapping multiple languages into a shared vector space using mBERT or XLM-R, we can train a model on English data and deploy it for Spanish, Arabic, or Swahili with surprising efficacy.

Dialectal Nuance

We examine the limitations of zero-shot transfer when dealing with regional dialects and slang, and how to mitigate performance drops using few-shot adapter tuning.
