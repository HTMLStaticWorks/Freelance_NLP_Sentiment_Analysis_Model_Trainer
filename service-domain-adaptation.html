Domain Adaptation

Cross-domain sentiment analysis presents unique challenges. Our methodology ensures high accuracy when migrating models from retail to finance or healthcare.

Feature Engineering

We identify "pivot" features that maintain their sentiment orientation across domains, while adapting domain-specific terminology to ensure the model remains robust in new linguistic environments.

Unsupervised Adaptation

Using adversarial training and coral alignment techniques, we can adapt models to a target domain using only unlabeled data, significantly reducing the need for new annotations.

Key Outcomes

  • Zero-shot transfer to related industry sectors
  • Significant reduction in domain-specific bias
  • Stable F1-scores across diverse dataset distributions
